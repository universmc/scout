Gantt Plan for Electoral Fraud Investigation

This Gantt plan presents the concept and development of the electoral fraud investigation project in a structured and organized manner. It highlights the main sections, tasks, and timeline for the project, adhering to the W3C semantic web standards.

Section 1: Research and Data Collection
Gathering Financial Documents
Collecting Testimonies and Evidence
Analyzing Data and Identifying Irregularities
Section 2: Legal Analysis and Compliance
Reviewing Relevant Laws and Regulations
Consulting Legal Experts and Authorities
Preparing Legal Documents and Reports
Section 3: Communication and Collaboration
Coordinating with Stakeholders and Authorities
Updating Progress and Sharing Findings
Section 4: Final Report and Recommendations
Compiling Findings and Writing the Report
Developing Recommendations and Next Steps
