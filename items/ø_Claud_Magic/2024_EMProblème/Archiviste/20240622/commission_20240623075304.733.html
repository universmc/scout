Evaluation Report: Horizon and Associated Groups

Review of Allegations Against Horizon

The review of allegations against Horizon reveals concerns about the usage of misleading terms, impacting electoral integrity and leading to possible accounting irregularities.

Implications of Parliamentary Dissolution by Macron

The dissolution of parliament by Macron has strengthened his majority at the expense of democratic values and opposition voices.

Internal Dynamics Between Different Factions Within Horizon

The internal dynamics between different factions within Horizon, notably EMP and Horizons, display mixed alignment and cohesion around shared goals and policy stances.

Application of EU's Horizon Europe Program and France 2030 Budget

The application of EU's Horizon Europe program and France 2030 budget shows a need for improved oversight and resource distribution towards innovation and technology development.

Balanced Assessment of Findings

This report presents a balanced assessment of the identified issues alongside commendable practices.

Practical Suggestions for Improvement

The following suggestions aim to enhance credibility, boost trustworthiness, promote transparent communication, and foster better integration among involved parties:

  • Implement clear and unambiguous terminology
  • Encourage democratic values and open dialogue
  • Strengthen internal cohesion between factions
  • Improve oversight and resource distribution
